Career Objectives FAQs

Posted 14 June 2022

There are some Frequently Asked Questions I am accumulating for future reference. This answers will be evolving as my career advances. I believe it is a good idea to place my answers to those questions in this space for all visitors of my webpage.

My top priorities and what I am optimizing for the next phase of my career.

STAYING RELEVANT
I am looking to work with elastic and dynamic infrastructure in order to be updated in the field of Data Science (ML/AI). Some corporations utilize their on-prem tech-stack to develop their projects-- a disadvantage because this won’t allow them as fast as open source solutions or cloud based infrastructure. Working with infrastructure designed by AWS, Microsoft, GOOGLE, is future proof, and readily available.

INCREASE MY RANGE OF KNOWLEDGE
Coding is an important part of my life: if learning a new coding language is part of the job, this is not a bad requirement for me. I am looking to accumulate as much knowledge as possible in coding languages and keep learning as much as possible about how the internet works.

BIGGER DATA
I want to stay in a domain where we handle Terabyte (or Petabyte) sized datasets. If it involves streaming data… even better. Big Data is a must for me. As we all know, big data means: Volume (very important for me), Velocity (important for me), and Veracity (disparate data and obfuscation is expected).

PAYMENTS
I want to keep learning about the payments industry. I believe it’s a very powerful area to specialize in. Eventually, and ideally I would want to be involved and become an expert in the areas of digital payments involving B2B, B2C, or P2P. It does not necessarily have to involve the exchange of money. The mere exchange of value between any two parties is an area that I find fascinating and will see a massive adoption in the future. Once the domain of micro-payments has been fully developed and prevalent, I will definitely want to be involved. At the moment I do want to keep developing the skills that will place me there. The economization of video gaming, the metaverse and similar futuristic techs will be ready soon along with its massive data needs. I am looking to prepare my ML skills to be capable of working on those domains.

LOGIC, MATHEMATICS, STATS and OPERATIONS RESEARCH
I am looking for opportunities which require use of logic and mathematics. I still intend to apply statistical concepts or optimization techniques-- areas I spent considerable time training on and I want to stay relevant.

What are some soft skills you are proud of having acquired in the last few years?

DIVERSITY
I had the luck to find work at Fortune 500 companies. It allowed me to meet intelligent peers with different backgrounds and cultures. This has enriched my career and helped me grow as a person. I have learned lessons beyond the science and engineering domain.

RESPECT
The best approach to team success has a foundation in the way every team member interacts with each other. Mutual respect, kindness and politeness is FIRST. Inclusion and tolerance are also essential qualities in any team I will work with.

COMMUNICATION
As a scientist, I also happen to be an introvert. Presentations where multiple people --especially executives-- can be challenging for people with my personality traits. I have improved in this area. Furthermore, LISTENING to what people's needs is important, and I have observed how other experts practice this skill.

DELIVERY
Shipping a successful product which satisfies the needs of end users is the achievement that gives me the most satisfaction, and ultimately lets me sleep at night. Delivering a successful product is what matters most to me.

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

A first priority for me is to stay on top of the technology involved in ML/AI Operations. It's important that I keep learning portable, open source, future proof skills. Tools such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Could are good candidates to stay relevant for the future of ML/AI.

At some point in the next 5 years, augmented reality will be functional and ubiquitous and thus exchange of data and value will be prevalent in the area. I would like to be involved in this domain. Data and Payments are evolving, and I want to help create the future solutions that will enable the embedding of AI into frictionless and ubiquitous exchange of data and value.There's nothing I will rather be doing than focusing my energies into driving Data and Value exchange into a more inclusive, robust, fast and fair system for everyone.
